About Us

We are Translas Logistics

Founded in 2017, Translas Logistics Company Limited is a licensed customs house agent, certified warehousing and haulage company that delivers on its commitment to provide world-class solutions to all clients with logistic needs.

We are guided by the factor of 3.

We deliver all consignments from vessel arrival within 3 days and we take pride in our feat.

Global Expertise
Working with a myriad network of global supply chain experts, we uphold the intricate principles of global standards in freight forwarding, logistics and warehousing services.
Prompt Delivery
Businesses today rely on timely and prompt execution of services. We understand how one minute can make all the difference in the value chain. We support our clients and enable them to stay ahead of the competition.
Sterling Customer Service
Understanding people is everything. Every customer, every partner, every organization behaves and operates differently. Proprietary service is the heart of our ethos – this makes us highly recommended in the logistics industry.
Our Services

What we provide

Fertilizer Distribution and Sales

We trade in the supply and delivery of Dangote urea fertilizer in Ghana, Burkina Faso, Togo, Ivory Coast.

Transportation and Distribution

This involves moving goods from one point to another. We also support our clients with product distribution solutions across Ghana and beyond.

Warehousing

We store and protect the goods for our clients in our network of warehouses. These facilities are equipped with electronic gates, remotely-monitored CCTV systems, fire alarms, smoke detectors, and emergency exits. They are also manned by a well-trained team of security service personnel and warehousing experts.

Frieght Forwarding

Translas covers the imports, exports, customs and delivery needs of its wide range of clients. This includes both medium and large scale requests from a manufacturer or producer to a market, customer or end-point of distribution.

Forklift Hire

We offer a number of forklift solutions to support varying gaps in the logistics process.

Packaging

Our packaging services extend to in-warehouse packaging for storage and distribution or packaging for transport and delivery.
